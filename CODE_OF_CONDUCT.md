# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Paper2Lab to stay collaborative, practical, and welcoming. Please be respectful, assume good intent, and focus feedback on the work rather than the person.

## Expected Behavior
- Be constructive and specific in issues and pull requests.
- Share context when reporting bugs or proposing changes.
- Respect different experience levels and communication styles.
- Prefer collaboration and clarification over blame.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Repeated bad-faith arguments or deliberate disruption.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if behavior harms the project or its contributors.
